Municipal Sewage Cleaning Machine Manufacturing Compnay of INDIA

Established in May 2000, McRAYGOR Mechanicals emerged as one of INDIA's largest manufacturers of Solid & Liquid Waste Handling Equipment specialising in Sewage Cleaning Machines. Ideology behind McRAYGOR was to fulfil the need for automating Sewage Treatment process and Eradicating Manual Scavenging in a country where this task has been traditionally carried out manually; thereby causing millions of deaths & hazardous diseases to sewage cleaning workers throughout the country. After years of hard work, innovation & technological optimization that could change how sewage problems in the country could be solved,

Metallurgically Researched And Yielded Gears On Radiant

McRAYGOR was founded.


Our machines – are a product of years of Engineering Excellence, Planning and Research & Development. Each Product undergoes various stages of testing & quality assurance by our team of world-class Engineers in unwavering determination to achieve perfection. All of our machines are manufactured in consonance to American & European Machinery Manufacturing standards; hence, a heavy amount of foreign exchequer is saved. Since our manufacturing technique is indigenous and suiting to the dynamic Indian requirements and environments, the cost factor has also been considered and it has been slashed by comparing with the foreign products. All Equipment are manufactured on latest state-of-the art technology conforming to related BIS/IS codes.

Our manufacturing plant is situated in Gurugram, Haryana (INDIA), spanning over 45,000 sq. ft. Our intelligent team consists of engineers who specialises in backend services such as Engineering & Design, Research & Development, Production Planning & Control, Purchase, Quality Assurance, Stores, Production, Commercial, Finance & a pan-India After Sales Service. As one of INDIA's oldest & Largest manufacturers of sewer cleaning machines, be rest assured that we have the solution to your every problem.
