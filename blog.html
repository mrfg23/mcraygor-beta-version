North Kashmir’s Kupwara has no garbage dumping site; waste openly thrown into rivers

Kupwara: This north Kashmir’s frontier district is without a single garbage dumping site and the waste material is being dumped either on roadsides or the water bodies. As per official record, about 11 tons of garbage is collected daily from main town Kupwara. This garbage is later dumped in water bodies or roadsides. The famous Lolab Valley in Kupwara is also the worst affected by the dumping of garbage in it. The Lolab river body, which flows from the higher ranges to main market Kupwara, is getting more polluted with each passing day.
